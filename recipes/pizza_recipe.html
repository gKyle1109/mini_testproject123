Kyle's Pizza recipe

This is just a normal pizza, the crunch of the crust and explosion of falavor when you bite into the pizza will have you coming back for more every time.

Pizza ingredients

  • Dough
  • sliced meat
  • tomato sauce
  • chesse

How to make Pizza

  1. Roll out dough into circle
  2. layer the tamato sauce, meat, and chesse
  3. cook until done
  4. add parmason and cut into triangles
