Kyle's sushi recipe

The rich fish combined with the vegtibals and rice form to make a perfect bite that will make you say WOW! Try it with soysause and wasibi for a morea authntic exprience.

Sushi ingredients

  • Rice
  • Fish
  • Seaweed
  • vegtibals

How to make sushi

  1. Cook the rice until its boiling
  2. Chop the vegtibals
  3. spread rice on the seaweed with vegtibals and rice it a line on top
  4. Roll the seaweed so the fish and vegtibals or in the middle
